How is TV CBD Possible?

Federal CBD Law Changes

TV CBD exists to serve the greater Treasure Valley area but operates safely in Oregon where it is possible to obtain a license to cultivate and handle Hemp products. The current laws based on Section 7606 of 2014 US Farm Bill allows cultivation of Hemp products but leaves some unanswered questions.
Introduced April 12th 2018 by Senator Mitch McConnell and Sponsored by Rep. James Comer, the Hemp Farming Act of 2018 would clear up legislation surrounding Hemp and CBD products. Check out more on McConnell's official page here.
