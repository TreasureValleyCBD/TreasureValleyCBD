You may be wondering how Treasure Valley CBD operates and exists with Idaho State Laws Prohibiting Cultivation and Sale of Hemp Products.
TV CBD exists to serve the greater Treasure Valley area but operates safely in Oregon awaiting Federal Law Changes that will allow us to sell our products in Idaho and all the 50 states.
Introduced April 12th 2018 by Senator Mitch McConnell and Sponsored by Rep. James Comer, the Hemp Farming Act of 2018 would change legislation that makes CBD flower and Oil Schedule 1 substances. Check out more on McConnell's official page here.
